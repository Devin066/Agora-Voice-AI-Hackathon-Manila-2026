# VisionVoice: One-Minute Pitch

## The Hook
**"What if your computer could be your eyes? VisionVoice turns any webcam into a smart assistant that speaks up to keep you safe."**

## Problem
285 million visually impaired people navigate daily life with limited environmental awareness. Traditional canes only detect ground-level obstacles, while existing tech solutions cost thousands and require special hardware. The result? Stressful navigation, preventable accidents, and constant dependence on others.

## Solution
VisionVoice is an AI-powered assistive copilot that transforms any webcam into smart glasses. It sees obstacles, recognizes people, and speaks concise guidance through natural voice interaction powered by Agora ConvoAI.

**Key Features:**
- **Proactive Alerts**: "Chair ahead, slightly left"
- **Person Recognition**: "Angela is in front of you"  
- **Voice Questions**: "What's in front?" → "Path is clear"
- **Caregiver Backup**: Optional human help when needed

## Demo Story (30 Seconds)

**[Start camera]**
*"I place a chair in front of the camera..."*
**Agora**: "Caution. Chair ahead, slightly left."

**[Teammate enters]**
*"My teammate Angela walks into frame..."*
**Agora**: "Angela is in front of you."

**[Voice interaction]**
*"I ask: What's in front of me?"*
**Agora**: "Chair ahead left, Angela in center, path clear right."

**[Emergency demo]**
*"User says: Call Angela"*
**System**: Initiates caregiver connection through Agora
