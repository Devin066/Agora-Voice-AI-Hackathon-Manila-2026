Safety Architecture
6 Layers of Safety.
Because Lives Depend On It.
1

Minimum Input Threshold

≥10 character requirement prevents accidental or empty triage submissions.

2

Hard-Coded RED Override

Blood, seizures, unconscious, breathing difficulty — always RED regardless of model output.

3

No False Reassurance

Patient mode: GREEN → YELLOW. We never downgrade. Uncertainty always escalates.

4

HITL Confirmation

"Sigurado ka ba?" confirmation card before any emergency bridge connection is opened.

5

BHW as Human Filter

Community health worker applies local knowledge before any escalation decision.

6

Device Rate Limiting

Maximum 3 RED alerts per device per day prevents abuse and alarm fatigue.

🚨 911 is always visible. On every screen. Always.
